General Information:

Transit of exoplanet HAT-P-51 b, with a host star at mag r 13.5 and strong Moon. The red data points show the light curve of the target star, in bins of 10 individual exposures of 40 s each. The error bars show the estimated 1-sigma uncertainty of the shot noise of the target star alone (ignoring in particular the background). The solid light curves show reference stars, with the same calibration as for the transit host star, and with arbitrary magnitude shifts (for display purposes). Vertical solid lines show expected ingress and egress times, and the horizontal lines show the expected depth of the transit. Data processing done with the data reduction tutorial.
