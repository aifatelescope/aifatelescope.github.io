General Information:

Transit of exoplanet HAT-P-23 b, host star at V mag around 11.9. The red data points show the light curve of the host star, in bins of 5 individual exposures of 40 s each. The error bars show the estimated 1-sigma uncertainty of the shot noise of the host star alone (i.e., not taking into account the shot noise from the background). The solid light curves show reference stars, with the same calibration as for the transit host star, and with arbitrary magnitude shifts (for display purposes). Vertical solid lines show expected ingress and egress times, and the horizontal lines show the expected depth of the transit. Data processing done with the data reduction tutorial.
